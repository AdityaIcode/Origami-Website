What if I don't have square paper?

This is a question that is asked alot. And I mean ALOT. You might have learnt a few tricks like folding a triangle and then cutting of the extra bits left over,
but this doesn't always end up with a accurate square. Some paper you can use are stuff like sticky notes. Sure, it will be harder to do more complicated origami,
but if you want to start of simple, it's better to have an accurate square.

Another way to get square paper, is to buy origami paper. This paper is specifically made to hold together and is not too small, or too big.
